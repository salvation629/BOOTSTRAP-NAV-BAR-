Frequently Asked Questions

Cryptocurrency is a type of digital or virtual currency that uses cryptography for security. It operates on decentralized networks, typically based on blockchain technology, and is not controlled by any central authority.

To buy cryptocurrency, create an account, complete the identity verification process, deposit funds (via bank transfer, credit card, or crypto), and place a buy order for your chosen coin or token.

Cryptocurrency investments carry risks due to price volatility and potential regulatory changes. While blockchain technology is secure, it's important to do your own research and only invest what you can afford to lose.
